Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CloudCFO.ai

How does AI processing work? +

CloudCFO.ai uses advanced GPT-4 technology combined with computer vision to extract data from your receipts and invoices with 99%+ accuracy. The AI understands context, handles multiple languages, and improves over time by learning from your corrections.

How do I set up WhatsApp and Telegram integration? +

Setting up is incredibly simple! Just scan a QR code or click a link to add our bot to your WhatsApp or Telegram. Once connected, you can send receipts just like you'd send a photo to a friend. Setup takes less than 2 minutes.

How does carbon accounting work? üå± +

CloudCFO.ai automatically calculates the carbon footprint for every transaction based on the vendor, category, and amount. We integrate with leading carbon accounting platforms like Persefoni, Watershed, and Sphera to provide accurate environmental impact data. This helps you meet ESG reporting requirements and marketplace sustainability standards.

Which accounting software do you integrate with? +

CloudCFO.ai integrates with all major accounting platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, FreshBooks, Wave, and over 30 other tools. We also connect with 12,000+ banks through Plaid for automatic reconciliation.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. CloudCFO.ai is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using 256-bit encryption. We store your documents securely in the cloud for 10 years to meet IRS requirements, and you maintain full control over access permissions.

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can process up to 100 documents during your trial to experience the full power of CloudCFO.ai.
